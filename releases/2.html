Raggi Laser

Damn Brain

Damn Brain seeks the absurd. But who is it and where is it located? Damn Brain is a melting unit which travels to the golden seasides of electroacoustic experimental music of the 1950s to acid-trip inspired oscillator sounds possibly heard while looking at a György Ligeti’s portrait puzzle with missing pieces, dissolving on the ground during a storm in downtown Berlin in 1996. Damn Brain is the echo of a thought, a consciousness teetering between the rational and the surreal. It has no defined body nor fixed identity, but it manifests in three entities, each a reflection of the others. It resides in a timeless place, a limbo where reality bends and the laws of physics dissolve. Here, among the waves of impossible geometries, Damn Brain roams, dancing to insistent notes and seeking meaning in the absurd, while impatient snails.

A.M. Davì - double neck bass guitar, percussions
Nicolò Masetto - piano, synth, ocarina, percussions
Manuel Scano - electronics, flutes
Art work by Manuel Scano
Record on tape and mastered during spring 2024 in Abano Terme (PD)
