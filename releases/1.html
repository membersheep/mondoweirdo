Fair Enough

Carboidrati Nightmare

Recorded on shitty 70s consumer tape decks, Fair Enough is a lo-fi experience you can play at your stoner parties.
In an age where the dance floor is no longer the place to hook up, Fair Enough will calm your emotions, it will lower your libido, it will keep you out of trouble. This is a mixtape that features different music improvised and played by a trio already known as "The Blue Men".
Nicolò Masetto on keys, Simone Coppiello on drums and A.M. Davì on bass. Working with the trio was a blast! Thank you guys!! They never complained about shitty cassette decks and crappy sound.
I call it Opium Funk or Hashish Disco.

Enjoy the listening!
